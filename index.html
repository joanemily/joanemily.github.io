INTRODUCTION

Joan Crookston

.NET Ninja Warrior

As an artist, my mission was to add depth to everyday connections and encounters. As a programmer, it remains unchanged. Whether in photography or EMS, I regularly took notice of details that are often overlooked by others - a skill that I carried with me into my work in administration. At C.C. Supply, I noticed that the system for scheduling and tracking deliveries was unreliable and chaotic. I updated it to streamline the information and make sure that it was available to the drivers even when I wouldn't be there. At Western Reserve Hospital Gastroenterology, I helped implement a process that assisted Pharmaceutical representatives when ordering lunches and saved them over hundreds of dollars while preventing food waste.

I have always prided myself on being clever and resourceful in working with and around systems to help companies save money, capture efficiencies, and eliminate confusion. Currently, I am studying .NET at Tech Elevator and I'm energized by the vast amount of new opportunities in the tech field that will allow me to reinforce my personal mission of serving others while challenging myself.
